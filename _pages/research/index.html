1. Vision-Based Intelligence & Lightweight Architecture Optimization

I design efficient vision models for real-time perception, focusing on reducing computational cost without sacrificing accuracy.
This includes lightweight backbones, optimized segmentation architectures, and deployment-friendly model redesign.

2. Multimodal Fusion for Industrial Safety

My research integrates visual, pose, and contextual signals to improve the reliability of safety monitoring systems.
Applications include PPE compliance detection, fall detection, hazard assessment, and zone-based risk estimation.

3. Real-Time Multi-Camera AI-COMS Systems

I develop scalable video analytics platforms capable of running multiple streams simultaneously.
AI-COMS is an end-to-end system that combines detection, segmentation, pose estimation, and alert generation for industrial safety environments.

4. Deepfake Detection & Multimodal Robustness

Beyond industrial AI, I explore generalization in deepfake and synthetic image detection, focusing on robustness across GAN, diffusion, and AI-generated media.
